ABOUT US



On 1925, this school was established as “David De SIlva Secondary Junior School”. Then it was transformed as, “David De Silva COllege” and in 1985.

Mrs. M J De Silva Hamine was the first Principal. On 13th February 1985, it was advanced as a Second Grade School. It had been a mixed school up to present. Since then, it had obtained successful results and achievements in both educational as well as in the extra-curricular activities. On 25th May 1993, it was upgraded to the national level.

Today there are about 700+ students receiving education in all disciplines of subjects which are included in the new Educational System. Grades 1-11 consists of 22 parallel classes. In Ordinary Level Section, students can select their scope of learning in Agriculture, Information Technology, Commerce or Arts streams. At the same time, subjects are taught with new amendments and the students are guided for exams successfully. We have scheduled new programmes to achieve the best results at the O/L examinations.
